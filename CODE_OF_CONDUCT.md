# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to provide a harassment-free experience for all, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience, education, socioeconomic status, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation, and to contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, and inclusive community.

## Our Standards
Positive behaviors:
- Empathy and kindness toward others
- Respect for differing opinions and experiences
- Graceful acceptance of constructive feedback
- Responsibility and apology for mistakes

Unacceptable behaviors:
- Sexualized language or advances
- Trolling, derogatory comments, and personal attacks
- Harassment (public or private)
- Sharing private information without consent

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Community leaders will enforce these standards, removing or moderating content that violates this Code of Conduct. They will provide reasons for moderation when appropriate.

## Scope
This Code applies to all community spaces and interactions, including public representations of the community (e.g., official emails, social media, events).

## Enforcement
Report abusive or unacceptable behavior to [founders@airasecurity.ai]. Complaints will be reviewed promptly and fairly while respecting the privacy of the reporter.

## Enforcement Guidelines
1. **Correction**: Private warning for inappropriate behavior.
2. **Warning**: Consequences for repeated behavior, including temporary exclusion.
3. **Temporary Ban**: Suspension from community interaction for a period.
4. **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from the community for severe violations.
